What is MUSIC TOGETHER?

Music is a universal language. It transcends boundaries and bonds people the world around. It reaches beyond culture and language to touch the depths of our souls, express our common emotions and inspires us all.


Music Together is an international early childhood music and movement program for children from birth through 5.

First offered to the public in 1987, it pioneered the concept of a research-based, developmentally appropriate early childhood music curriculum that strongly emphasizes and facilitates adult involvement. Now serving 2,500 communities in over 40 countries, Music Together connects families and communities across the globe as they express and explore the basic human instinct for making music.
